Background

I went to school for film/tv and Spanish. Sooner after graduation, I moved to LA to follow my dreams. And after a few years of working in the industry, I left to pursue my other love: education. I established Roble Education with my best friend and business partner a few months later.

Take a look at our website at roble.education and follow us on social media.
