Hello, I'm Andy.

New York is my home.

Background

I'm a filmmaker and an educator, who is in awe of his own ignorance about the web. In fact, the act of writing this very line and having it appear on a computer is, well, awe-inspiring. I'm kinda like Lenny from Of Mice and Men, in that way. Or more like Biff Loman, given that he was aware of his own shortcomings. How do I delete the part about Lenny?

I run a company, named Roble Education, that works with schools and universities to produce in-classroom and online learning experiences. Check it out: roble.education.

Goals

I want to make friends at dinner parties by telling them that I have fake coding skills in languages like:

  • HTEML
  • CESES
  • CoffeeScript
  • Diamond
  • Trains

But mostly I hope to increase my understanding of the internet. Indeed, basic knowledge of the web is important for everybody in the 21st century.
